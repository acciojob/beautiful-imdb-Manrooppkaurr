Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani is a 2013 Indian romantic comedy film about the coming-of-age of four friends. The film follows the story of Bunny (Ranbir Kapoor) and Naina (Deepika Padukone), who meet on a trekking trip and fall in love, but Bunny leaves India to pursue his career before Naina can express herself. They meet again years later, but he still values his dreams more than their bond.
